Use cron job: 
You can have notifications being sent by a cron job by executing
the send_notifications.php script located in the www/
folder of your installation. However, it is essential that you define
the HTTP_HOST variable for the $_SERVER variable.
A sample script that could be executed by cron is the following:

<?php
$_SERVER['HTTP_HOST'] = "127.0.0.1";
include "/home/user/eFront/www/send_notifications.php";
?>
